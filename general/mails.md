Learning Objectives

Practical Scrum Mastery: Students will gain a deep understanding of Scrum principles, roles, events, and artifacts by actively participating in 4 sprints with a length of 90 minutes, allowing them to apply Scrum concepts in a real-world scenario.

Effective Agile Collaboration: Through hands-on experience, students will learn the importance of collaboration, communication, and teamwork within a Scrum framework, enabling them to work more effectively in cross-functional development teams.

Time Management and Prioritization: Students will develop essential time management skills as they navigate the tight sprint schedules, helping them prioritize tasks and make informed decisions on what to work on during each sprint.

Continuous Improvement: The course will instill a culture of continuous improvement by encouraging students to reflect on their sprint performance, identify areas for enhancement, and implement changes in subsequent sprints.

Adaptability and Flexibility: Through the rapid iteration of one-hour sprints, students will become adept at adapting to changing requirements and unexpected challenges, preparing them for the dynamic nature of real-world software development projects.

These learning outcomes will ensure that participants not only grasp Scrum principles but also develop essential soft skills and a mindset conducive to successful agile development practices
